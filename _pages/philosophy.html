Philosophy

My core beliefs regarding software engineering, product design, and continuous learning.

Iterative Over Perfection

Shipping a functional prototype early provides more value than a perfect system shipped too late. Gather feedback, adapt, and refine iteratively.

Simplicity is a Feature

Complex systems fail in complex ways. Keeping architectural components simple and loosely coupled prevents technical debt and cognitive overload.

The User Comes First

Every engineering decision should ultimately trace back to improving the experience for the end-user. Performance and accessibility are not optional.
